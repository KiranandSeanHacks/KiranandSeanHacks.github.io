Mission

Our mission here at RainDropStopDrop is to help you stop your drop so you can make it rain. Did you know that the average North American uses about 300L of water every day? That's about one bathtub full of water for every person, everyday. With our planet's diminishing sources of fresh water, our overconsumption of the liquid of life is one of the bigger problems of our time. RainDropStopDrop has partnered with brands offering water saving appliances to make it easier and cheaper for you to save water.

How?

By using the link on our website to order our partners' products, you'll be offered discounts, making it more affordable to purchase water saving appliances. You'll obviously also save money using these products, as they'll reduce the amount of water you use, saving you money on your hydro bill.
